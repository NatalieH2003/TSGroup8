Rules and Directions

Blackjack

Blackjack is a classic card game where the goal is to beat the dealer by getting as close to 21 as possible without going over.

  • Each player starts with two cards. Face cards are worth 10, and Aces can be 1 or 11.
  • Players can choose to "Hit" (take another card) or "Stand" (keep their current total).
  • If your total exceeds 21, you bust and lose the round.

Euchre

Euchre is a team-based card game where players compete to win tricks.

  • The game is played with a deck of 24 cards (9 through Ace).
  • Players bid on the trump suit, which dominates all others.
  • Points are scored based on the number of tricks won by the bidding team.

Poker

Poker is a strategic card game involving betting, bluffing, and hand ranking.

  • Players aim to form the best five-card hand using their hole cards and community cards.
  • Hand rankings: Royal Flush, Straight Flush, Four of a Kind, Full House, Flush, Straight, Three of a Kind, Two Pair, One Pair, High Card.
  • Bets are placed in rounds: pre-flop, flop, turn, and river.

Roulette

Roulette is a casino game where players bet on where a ball will land on a spinning wheel.

  • The wheel has 38 slots (1-36, 0, and 00).
  • Bets can be placed on individual numbers, groups, colors, or odd/even.
  • Winnings are based on the odds of the selected bet.
Back to Roulette

Texas Hold'em

Texas Hold'em is a popular poker variation where players use two hole cards and five community cards to form their best hand.

  • Each player is dealt two hole cards face down.
  • Five community cards are revealed in phases: flop (3 cards), turn (1 card), and river (1 card).
  • Players bet in four rounds: pre-flop, flop, turn, and river.

Slots

Slots is a simple and fun game where you spin reels to match symbols and win rewards.

  • Choose your bet amount and select lines to bet on (rows or columns).
  • Click the "Spin" button to start the reels.
  • Match symbols on the selected lines to win.
  • The payout depends on the number of matched symbols and the line bet.
